About Us


Coffee Racer Customs is a team of motorcycle enthusiast with a passion for building motorcycles.
Established at 2019, we started at Eiji's garage customizing each other's motorcycle until people showed interest and asked us who built our bikes.
We noticed that there are only few people who knows how to customize bikes into classic flavor. Since then we started accepting clients around the city to brew the style they want for their bikes.
